02 - Homeless Help

Project introduction

For this project my role was to lead the app and do the UX Design.

The goal of this project was to design an app that will improve communication and help communities to come together and find those who are in need.

Considering the target audience, the app needed to be easy to use.

We also needed to make the process fast because people didn't had much time.

Research study details

Our reseach questions were :

  • "How can we make users more incline to help others"
  • "What can we learn from the steps that users take to find communities events"

For the study we had 5 participants : 2 males, 2 females and 1 non-binary individual who were between the age of 18 and 50.

The methodology was the following : 5 minutes per participant, New York and remote, with an unmoderated usability study. Users were asked to follow a prompt with a low fidelity prototype.

Wireframes

User testing result

The following themes emerged from the user testing :

  • The navigation is not intuive
  • The workflow was complicated
  • Users didn't know what to do

To correct those points we added a homepage to list all the available actions of the app. Also we added maps to indicate events position

High fidelity designs

Conclusions

Even though the app had a small number of pages, making an intuitive navigation was not that simple.

People were getting more involved when the act of helping others was simplified with the app.

For the future we need to add charity donations directly through the app and improver event's management. If people could create events through the app it will be event simpler.
